Nutrition Tips

Eating well is the foundation of good health. Here are some simple tips:

  • Eat more fruits and vegetables
  • Choose whole grains over refined
  • Stay hydrated with water

Exercise Routine

Regular movement keeps your body strong:

  1. Aim for 30 minutes daily
  2. Mix cardio and strength training
  3. Don't forget to stretch

Sleep Well

Quality sleep is essential for health:

  • 7-9 hours for adults
  • Keep a consistent schedule
  • Dark, quiet, cool bedroom
